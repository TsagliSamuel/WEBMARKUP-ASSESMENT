What the Netherlands is famous for

The netherlands is one of the famous cities for tourists to visit. Every year about 16 million tourists visits the Netherlands for the good experience the country provides. The netherlands is known for its tulips, windmills, cheese, wooden shoes, canals and mostly bicycles. The netherlands is also one of the countries in the world with the most bicycles , also called the city with too many bicycles. This makes the country one of the special countries in the world.A lot of free bikes are offered to tourists as well.

The Cities

The netherlands has about 23 beautiful cities and with the capital being Amsterdam, which is the touristy part of the Netherlands. Amsterdam is famous for its canals , beautiful houses , 'coffee' shops and the Red Light District. There are a lot of activities to do as a tourist in Amsterdam such as walking along the beautiful canals , renting a bicycle or just simply visiting the amazing restaurants and bars in the city.And because of this, there are a lot of promotions such as 'free weed' and special cards for tourists who visit Amsterdam and certain cities during the summer.

Rijksmuseum

The Rijksmuseum is a state museum in Amsterdam that belongs to the most breathtaking museums in the world.It is the most visited museum in the Netherlands. It houses over 1 million piece of artwork, documents and other important items associated with Dutch history. They got a couple masterpiece such as the Rembrandt's ,The Night Watch and others such as The Milkmaid and several paintings by Vincent van Gogh.

Know more about the Rijksmuseum
The Museumplein

The Museumplein is the most popular tourist spot in Amsterdam. It has this name because its located between two of the biggest museums in Amsterdam ; the Rijksmuseum and the Concertgebouw. The area got the name because of the museums located around the square and therefore also referred to as Museum Quarter or Museum District. Almost all tourists in Amsterdam eventually end up on the Museumplein. Apart from the fact that three of the city’s most popular museums in Amsterdam are located there, the square also has some canal cruise companies in the vicinity. There are trendy shops for shopaholics, cosy restaurants serving tasty dishes from all over the world and in the summer, lots of sporting activities are also organised on the square. The popular Leidseplein is also just a mere 5-minute walk from the Museumplein.

Know more about the Museumplein
The Johan Cruijff Arena

The Johan Cruijff Arena is the main and the largest stadium in the Netherlands. This legendary stadium was renamed after the late Johan Cruijff who played for the Barcelona FC . Both dutch and international football events are held at this stadium each year and therefore is visited by a large number of people with each football match or even a music concert. The stadium can hold up to 55,000 people during football matches.
